Title: Password Reset Help
Category: Account Access

Problem description:
Customers may be unable to sign in because they forgot their password, did not receive a password reset email, or the reset link has expired. This document explains how to help users reset their password safely.

Symptoms:
- Customer cannot log in with their current password.
- Customer says the password reset email has not arrived.
- Customer receives an expired or invalid reset link message.
- Customer is locked out after multiple failed login attempts.
- Customer is unsure which email address is linked to the account.

Troubleshooting steps:
1. Ask the customer to confirm they are using the correct login email address.
2. Ask them to use the Forgot Password option from the login page.
3. Ask them to check spam, junk, promotions, and corporate email quarantine folders.
4. Confirm that the reset link was opened from the most recent email only.
5. Ask the customer to try the reset link in an incognito/private browser window.
6. Ask the customer to clear browser cache or try another browser.
7. If the account is locked, wait for the lockout period to expire or escalate to support.
8. Never ask the customer to share their current or new password.

Required information:
- Account email address.
- Approximate time the reset was requested.
- Error message shown, if any.
- Browser and device used.
- Whether the customer has access to the registered email inbox.

When to escalate:
- Customer cannot access the registered email address.
- Password reset emails are not being delivered after multiple attempts.
- Customer reports suspicious account activity.
- Account appears locked, suspended, or compromised.
- Customer is an enterprise admin or high-priority customer.

Suggested customer response:
Hi, I can help you reset your password. Please use the Forgot Password option on the login page and make sure you open the latest reset email. Also check your spam or junk folder in case the email was filtered. For security reasons, please do not share your password with us. If the reset email still does not arrive, send us your account email address, the time you requested the reset, and any error message you see so we can investigate further.
